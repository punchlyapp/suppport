üîß Getting Started

Account Setup

Download Punchly from the App Store, create an account with email or sign in with Google, complete your profile setup, and start creating your first project.

Creating Your First Project

Tap the "+" button in Projects tab, enter project name and description, set project location (optional), and invite team members if needed.

Adding Punch List Items

Open your project, tap "+" to add new punch item, fill in description and details, take photos and add annotations, set priority and assign to team member, then save the punch item.

Managing Team Members

Go to project settings, tap "Invite Team Members", enter email addresses, set permissions for each member, and send invitations.

üìã Frequently Asked Questions

Is Punchly free to use? +
Yes, Punchly offers a free plan with 1 project, 50 punches per month, and 3 team members. Premium plans are available for larger projects and teams.
Does Punchly work offline? +
Yes, Punchly works fully offline. All data syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet.
Can I export my data? +
Yes, you can export punch lists as PDF reports. Pro plans include clean, professional PDFs without watermarks.
How do I upgrade my subscription? +
Go to Settings > Subscription Management to view and upgrade your plan.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel anytime through your App Store account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits? +
You'll be prompted to upgrade when you reach your limits. Your existing data remains safe.
How do I sync my data across devices? +
Sign in with the same account on all devices. Data syncs automatically when connected to the internet.
What if I lose my device? +
Your data is safely stored in the cloud. Simply sign in on a new device to access all your projects.

üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

App won't sync:

  1. Check internet connection
  2. Force close and reopen app
  3. Sign out and sign back in
  4. Contact support if issue persists

Photos not uploading:

  1. Check storage space on device
  2. Ensure camera permissions are enabled
  3. Try taking new photos
  4. Restart the app

Can't invite team members:

  1. Verify email addresses are correct
  2. Check if invitee already has an account
  3. Ensure you have team member slots available
  4. Contact support for assistance
